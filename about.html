About Black Box Eatz

Black Box Eatz is presented here as a highly popular, Black-owned Caribbean and Southern soul food fusion restaurant located in Brooklyn, New York.

The design uses warm colors, bold text, and a modern layout to match the restaurant’s strong personality. The goal is to make visitors feel the energy of the food before they even walk in.

Design Choices

I used a dark background, orange/gold accents, rounded cards, and simple navigation to create a website that feels professional, flavorful, and easy to use.
